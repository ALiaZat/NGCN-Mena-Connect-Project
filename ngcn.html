About MENA Connect

MENA Connect is a networking website with the intent of bridging the professional gap between students and experts in a range of career fields. The professional fields represented in MENA Connect are diverse and span across multiple disciplines, creating a comprehensive platform. Field experts are invited to join MENA Connect if they have an active desire to interact with and mentor beginners in that area of study, so each connection a student makes is guaranteed to result in a strong networking opportunity. Networking will give students the opportunity to learn about their field of interest in a one-on-one virtual setting and has the potential to provide students with internships or job shadowing opportunities.
